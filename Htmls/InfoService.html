A short haircut is characterized by its close-cropped length, typically reaching just above the ears or at the nape of the neck. This style often exudes a sleek and modern look, accentuating facial features and providing a low-maintenance option for those seeking a chic and effortless appearance. Short haircuts can vary in style, ranging from pixie cuts to cropped bobs, offering versatility and ease of styling for individuals of all ages and hair types.
